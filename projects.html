MOSFET I-V Characteristics Experiment

Ongoing

This project focuses on analyzing the current-voltage characteristics of a MOSFET and connecting measured device behavior to semiconductor theory.

I am studying how current changes under different operating conditions and how this behavior relates to concepts such as cutoff, triode, and saturation regions. The goal is to better understand semiconductor device operation not only mathematically, but also from a physical engineering perspective.

Skills / Concepts:

  • Semiconductor device fundamentals
  • Experimental analysis
  • Electrical measurement and interpretation
  • Connecting theory with real device behavior
