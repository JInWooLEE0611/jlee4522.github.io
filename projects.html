Projects

Selected academic and engineering projects

MOSFET I-V Characteristics Experiment

This project focuses on analyzing the current-voltage characteristics of a MOSFET and connecting measured behavior to semiconductor device theory. The goal is to better understand how carrier transport, operating conditions, and device parameters affect electrical performance.

Through this project, I aim to strengthen my understanding of semiconductor fundamentals while also practicing experimental analysis and technical communication.

Digital System Design Project

This project involved designing and analyzing a digital system using logic-based methods. It helped me better understand how hardware logic is structured, optimized, and implemented in a practical engineering setting.

The project strengthened my ability to approach hardware problems systematically and to connect abstract design logic with real engineering implementation.

Solar Panel Monitoring System

In this project, I worked on a system related to monitoring electrical output in a solar panel setup. It gave me experience thinking about measurement, signal interpretation, and the relationship between hardware behavior and real-world system performance.

This project also helped me build confidence in presenting engineering work through clear explanation and structured documentation.
